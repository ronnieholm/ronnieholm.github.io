Bugfree Consulting specializes in delivering high-quality, professionally built software. No hacks or shortcuts, just software that adds business value. Solutions are built in close collaboration and by delivering early and often.

Services

We facilitate requirement gathering workshops and prototype high-risk or uncertain pieces first to strike the right balance between functionality, simplicity, and maintainabilty. We strive to implement solutions using short cycles and close customer collaboration.

Technologies

We mostly develop native or web-based solutions for the Windows platform using a mix of .NET, C#, F#, ASP.NET MVC, SharePoint/Office 365, and SQL server, but also have experience with Linux-based solutions.

Products

We sell a product to synchronize local filesystems or network shares with SharePoint document libraries, intended for organizations who want to leverage the versioning, collaboration, and search aspects of SharePoint, yet aren't ready to transition away from regular files and folders.
